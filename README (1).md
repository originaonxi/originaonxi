## What Aonxi actually is

Most revenue tools are databases pretending to be engines. Aonxi is a living revenue engine — 120+ AI persona models deployed as active workflows, 76 enterprise-grade intent filters, native human-in-the-loop control nodes, and 100M+ daily enriched B2B records flowing through real-time pipelines.

Every workflow ships as an isolated pilot. It ingests live signals, applies reasoning, routes edge cases to humans, and tracks downstream attribution — pipeline velocity, closed-won dollars, ROI. Positive performers auto-replicate. Losers get terminated with zero drama. You only pay for what prints money.

**The moat:**

- 76 enterprise-grade live intent filters and controls
- Human-in-the-loop nodes natively integrated — genuine human-AI symbiosis, not an add-on

## What I think about

**Data quality as a moat.** The models have their labs. The chips have their fabs. Data has been treated as an afterthought — raw, noisy, unstructured. The next era of AI progress depends on methodological rigor around data. Better data beats better algorithms, every time.

**Agentic systems that close the loop.** Not AI that suggests — AI that executes. Identifies the right decision-maker, sends the right message at the right moment, routes the exception to a human, tracks whether it converted. The whole loop, automated, auditable.

**AI at population scale.** Beyond B2B: I think about what AI infrastructure looks like when the users are not enterprise sales teams but 900 million people who have never had access to any of this. India's ABHA, UPI, ONDC, Bhashini — a new stack is being built and I want to build on top of it.
